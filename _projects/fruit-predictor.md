A fruit classification system that uses computer vision to automatically detect whether fruits are fresh or rotten. The model analyzes visual features such as color, texture, and surface condition from input images and classifies each fruit accordingly. It is designed to help with quick quality inspection, reducing the need for manual checking and improving consistency in identifying spoiled or fresh produce.

It can be used in two flexible modes: users can either upload a photo of a fruit or use a live webcam feed for real-time detection. This makes it suitable for both quick checks of individual images and continuous, real-time monitoring.

Fruits condition that can be detected : banana, apple, strawberry

[Try the app](https://fruit-freshness-detector-laurapa.streamlit.app/)
