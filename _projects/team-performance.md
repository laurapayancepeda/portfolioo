This project leverages StatsBomb data to analyze player and team performance during the UEFA EURO 2024 season using RStudio. It ranks teams by shooting volume, focusing on total shot output, and examines the attacking output of the top 20 players (minimum 360 minutes played) through metrics such as shots and key passes per 90 minutes.

The visualization also explores player progression into advanced areas, including completed passes into the final third, with a particular emphasis on Toni Kroos’s influence in build-up play and ball distribution.
