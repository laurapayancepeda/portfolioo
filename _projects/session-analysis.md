The objective of this Power BI dashboard is to analyze one month of GPS and wellness data to evaluate Real Madrid’s training session outcomes, monitor individual athlete wellness scores, and assess recent performance trends. This enables stakeholders and coaching staff to efficiently identify potential areas of concern or improvement in player performance and recovery.

The dashboard currently includes a Daily Session Report, an Individual Player Report, and a Player Wellness Report.
