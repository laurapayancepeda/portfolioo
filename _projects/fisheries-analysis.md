Developed an interactive Tableau dashboard to analyse Lake Powell fisheries data (2004–2018), revealing key trends in environmental conditions, fish populations, and overall ecosystem health. Additionally, built a Random Forest model in RapidMiner to predict fish weight based on stomach content features, achieving an overall accuracy of 56.91%, with high precision for lighter fish (94.77%) and strong recall for heavier fish (99.19%).

This project demonstrates the application of data-driven modelling to support fisheries management, ecological research, and conservation initiatives, enabling more effective monitoring and preservation of aquatic ecosystems.
