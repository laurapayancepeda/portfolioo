This Tableau dashboard provides an in-depth analysis of Liverpool’s performance in the 2019 UEFA Champions League Final, leveraging detailed StatsBomb event data. It examines a range of advanced metrics, including pass maps to visualize ball distribution and player connectivity, shot locations and shot quality to assess attacking threat, passing accuracy and sequences to evaluate build-up play, and average player positions to understand team shape and spatial organization.

The dashboard is designed to uncover insights into Liverpool’s tactical approach, highlighting patterns in possession, transitions, and chance creation, as well as individual player contributions within the overall system. By combining multiple visual layers, it offers a comprehensive and data-driven view of how the match unfolded.
