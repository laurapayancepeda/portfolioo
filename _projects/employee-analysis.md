This Tableau dashboard delivers a comprehensive and structured view of employees across a company’s branches and headquarters, enabling both high-level oversight and detailed individual analysis. It provides in-depth breakdowns of workforce demographics, including attributes such as age, gender, education level, and departmental distribution, alongside detailed salary insights to support compensation analysis and benchmarking.

The dashboard also incorporates an interactive summary table that consolidates key employee information—such as name, job title, location, salary, employment status, and length of service—into a single, easy-to-navigate view. This table is fully searchable and filterable by employee identifier, allowing users to quickly locate specific individuals and drill down into their profiles.

Designed with usability in mind, the dashboard supports dynamic filtering across multiple dimensions, enabling users to explore trends, compare branches, and analyze workforce composition efficiently. Overall, it serves as a powerful tool for HR analytics, workforce planning, and data-driven decision-making.
