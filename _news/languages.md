I am a multilingual individual with a strong interest in languages and cross-cultural communication.

My native language is Spanish. Growing up in a Spanish-speaking environment has given me a strong foundation in communication, expression, and cultural awareness.

I am fluent in English, which I use confidently in academic and professional contexts. English has played a key role in my education and has enabled me to engage with international environments and global perspectives.

I am currently at HSK3 level in Chinese and actively still learning. I am driven by a deep interest in Chinese culture. I continue to develop my skills with consistency and enthusiasm as part of my long-term learning journey.

Additionally, living in the Gulf has enriched me with a deep understanding of many Middle Eastern cultures. I also have an elementary level of Arabic, with the ability to read and write basic texts. I am actively building on this foundation as I continue to expand my language abilities.
