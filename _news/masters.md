During my Master’s programme, I developed a strong interdisciplinary understanding of data-driven business, digital transformation, and enterprise systems. The course combined technical data science skills with strategic and managerial perspectives, allowing me to understand how organisations leverage data, technology, and digital infrastructure to improve decision-making and operational efficiency. Throughout the programme, I worked across both analytical and business-focused disciplines, gaining experience in areas such as machine learning, big data systems, digital strategy, logistics, and enterprise resource planning.

A significant part of my learning focused on applying data analytics and computational methods to real-world business problems. I built skills in data mining, predictive modelling, and big data management, which enabled me to work with large and complex datasets and extract meaningful insights to support decision-making. Alongside this technical development, I explored how organisations adopt digital technologies, manage transformation initiatives, and align IT systems with broader business goals. Modules in project and portfolio management further strengthened my ability to structure, plan, and deliver complex projects while balancing scope, risk, and stakeholder expectations.

My Master’s provided a solid grounding in both the theoretical and practical aspects of big data systems and management, preparing me to effectively bridge the gap between data, technology, and decision-making.

## Modules and Key Learnings

- **Data Mining and Machine Learning**
  - Predictive modelling and pattern recognition
  - Supervised and unsupervised learning techniques

- **Digital Business Technology and Management**
  - Digital transformation and organisational change
  - Technology adoption in business environments
  - Managing IT-enabled business processes

- **Project and Portfolio Management in Organisations**
  - Project lifecycle and delivery frameworks
  - Portfolio prioritisation and governance
  - Risk and stakeholder management

- **Big Data Management**
  - Data architecture and storage systems
  - Data processing pipelines and governance
  - Managing large-scale structured and unstructured data

- **Digital Strategy**
  - Digital business models and competitive advantage
  - Platform ecosystems and digital disruption
  - Aligning strategy with technology adoption

- **Logistics and Physical Distribution**
  - Supply chain design and optimisation
  - Distribution and operations management
  - Inventory and logistics planning

- **Enterprise Systems and SAP**
  - ERP systems and business process integration
  - Cross-functional enterprise operations
  - System-driven organisational efficiency

- **Big Data Analytics for Business**
  - Business intelligence and data visualisation
  - Analytical techniques for decision-making
  - Translating data insights into business value

- **MSc Project**
  - Independent research design and execution
  - Data collection and analytical methods
  - Applied problem-solving in a real-world context
